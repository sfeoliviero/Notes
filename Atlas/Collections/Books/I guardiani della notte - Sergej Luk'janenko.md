# I guardiani della notte

## Summary
"I guardiani della notte" (The Night Watch) by Sergei Lukyanenko is an urban fantasy novel set in modern-day Moscow. It introduces a world where supernatural beings like vampires, werewolves, and magicians exist alongside humans. The story follows Anton Gorodetsky, a member of the Night Watch, an organization tasked with maintaining the balance between the forces of Light and Dark. The novel explores moral ambiguity, power struggles, and the complexities of good and evil.
