# Celtika. Il codice di Merlino

## Summary
"Celtika: Il codice di Merlino" by Robert Holdstock is a fantasy novel that blends Celtic mythology with Arthurian legend. The story follows Merlin as he uncovers ancient secrets tied to the mystical forests of Celtic Britain. The novel explores themes of magic, destiny, and the intersection of myth and history, offering a richly imagined world where legendary figures confront forgotten powers.
