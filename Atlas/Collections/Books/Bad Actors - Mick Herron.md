# Bad Actors

## Summary
"Bad Actors" by Mick Herron is a spy thriller and the seventh installment in the Slough House series. The novel follows a group of disgraced MI5 agents who are relegated to a dead-end department known as Slough House. When a former colleague is kidnapped, the team must navigate a web of deceit, betrayal, and international intrigue to uncover the truth. Herron's sharp wit and intricate plotting make this a gripping exploration of espionage and redemption.
