# Vita Nova. Eternal war

## Summary
*Vita Nova* is part of the Eternal War science fiction series by Italian author Livio Gambarini. Set in a dystopian future, the novel explores themes of war, survival, and human resilience in a world torn apart by endless conflict. The story follows characters navigating political intrigue and moral dilemmas in a society where peace seems impossible.
