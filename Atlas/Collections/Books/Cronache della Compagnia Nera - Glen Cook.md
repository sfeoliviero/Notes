# Cronache della Compagnia Nera

## Summary
"Cronache della Compagnia Nera" (The Black Company) by Glen Cook is a seminal work in the dark fantasy genre. The series follows a mercenary group known as the Black Company as they navigate a world filled with magic, war, and moral ambiguity. Through the eyes of the Company's annalist, Croaker, the novels explore themes of loyalty, survival, and the cost of violence, offering a gritty and realistic take on fantasy storytelling.
