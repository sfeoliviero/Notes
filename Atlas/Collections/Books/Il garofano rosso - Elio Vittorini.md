# Il garofano rosso

## Summary
"Il garofano rosso" (The Red Carnation) by Elio Vittorini is a coming-of-age novel set in Sicily during the early 20th century. It follows the story of a young boy as he navigates the complexities of adolescence, love, and political awakening. The novel captures the tension between personal desires and societal expectations, offering a poignant portrayal of youth and rebellion in a changing world.

La storia di un liceale e del suo amore timido per una compagna di scuola. Ma anche l'analisi di una maturazione sentimentale, politica e sociale nell'Italia degli anni Venti.
