## Key Features

### Race: Variant Human

- **+1 WIS, +1 CON** (optimized for spellcasting and durability)
- **Feat:** **War Caster**
  - Advantage on CON saves for concentration
  - Cast spells with hands full
  - Opportunity attacks with spells

### Peace Domain Features

1. **Implement of Peace (Level 1)**
   - Proficiency in **Persuasion**
   - **Emboldening Bond (6/day):** Bonus action to grant d6 to ally's attack/save/skill

2. **Channel Divinity: Balm of Peace (2/short rest)**
   - Heal 2d6+5 HP and end one *charmed* or *frightened* effect

3. **Protective Bond (Level 6)**
   - Reaction to impose disadvantage on attack against ally

4. **Potent Spellcasting (Level 8)**
   - Add WIS mod (+5) to cantrip damage

5. **Divine Intervention (Level 10)**
   - 50% chance to call for divine aid (1/week)
